Welcome to the FAQ! Scroll through this page to answer some frequently asked questions!

What made you start fansubbing?

Our group initally started fansubbing Seven Deadly Sins: Four Knights of the Apocalypse because netflix does not release offical subtitles until they release the dub. This means that for months the fandom would either have almost no idea what's being said or even a lack of being able to watch the episodes. That's where we come in and become the middle-man of sorts, providing subtitles where netflix wont.

How do i view the episodes?

There are two ways of viewing our subs that we support. These are either downloading from our release page or watching via our discord.

What do you think of X site uploading your subs?

While we do not recommend watching via these methods for a variety of reasons the rules of the seven sea's say these sites will exist no matter what we do.
